Ego Influence : Impact on Business Ratings or not

Yelp is a crowd sourced local business review and social networking site, where users post their reviews about a business using a star rating system on a scale of one to five.

Our research is based on two things; firstly trying to understand the factors that affect the ratings that a business gets and secondly, since Yelp is a social network in itself, we try to understand how a user who has reviewed a business is influenced by his ego network and if that too could be one of the reasons why a person would prefer going to a restaurant. Often businesses like restaurants take paper based feedback from the customers that visit them. We use the online reviews from Yelp and train a model on it, through which we predict if a user would review or give feedback for a business based on the users friend opinions.

This study is one of the first ones to explore the social network in a website like Yelp. In our research, we define a term Ego Influence as the combination of the following factors:

  • Social Influence:

    • This feature is a measure of to what extent a user's action/behavior of visting a business (restaurant) is influenced by his/her social network. If most the user's friends visit a joint then would the user also visit that place ?

  • Social Sentiment

    • This feature goes a step beyond the first one. It takes into account the opinions of a user's online friend circle and predicts whether the user would visit a place depending on what the user's friend have to say about that place. The opinion could be a positive one or a negative one depending on their reviews.

  • Closeness with friends in network

    • This feature gives us another important way to measure whether a user would visit a place depending on how close is she with her network. We define the Personalized Page Rank value of a user to be the closeness of user to her network.

Research Questions:

1. Given an ego nodes sentiment score (for a business), his personalized page rank (Closeness) and social influence measure, can we predict if a user has given review or not.


2. What factors influence a persons visit to a restaurant ?

We derived some significant results from our study:

  • Our regression model suggests that Closeness with your friends (Personalized Page Rank) is a very strong factor that helps predicting the ratings that a business receives.
  • Besides closeness; Price, Average User review count and number of fans that a user has also form good predictors.
  • Our classifier gives 68% accuracy for predicting whether a user will review a restaurant or not depending on influence of his friends.
